## Welcome to Flow Browser

When you first launch Flow Browser, you'll be guided through a quick onboarding process to personalize your browsing experience. This process takes less than a minute and helps you configure the browser to match your preferences.

## Onboarding Stages

The onboarding wizard consists of several stages that guide you through the essential setup:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Welcome Screen">
    You'll be greeted with the Flow Browser logo and a welcome message. Click **Get Started** to begin the setup process.
    
    This screen introduces you to Flow Browser and prepares you for the customization steps ahead.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Space">
    Spaces are Flow Browser's unique way of organizing your browsing sessions. During onboarding, you'll create your initial space through three sub-steps:

    ### Name Your Space
    Choose a name for your first space (e.g., "Work", "Personal", "Research").

    ### Choose a Space Icon
    Select an icon that visually represents your space. Flow Browser provides a variety of icon options to choose from.

    ### Select Space Colors
    Customize your space with a color scheme. The colors you choose will be applied to the space's theme, making it easy to identify at a glance.

    <Info>
      Spaces help you separate different browsing contexts. You can create additional spaces later from the settings.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Browser Icon">
    Personalize Flow Browser by choosing your preferred application icon. 

    - Browse through available icon designs created by various artists
    - Select the icon that best matches your style
    - The selected icon will be applied to your system immediately

    <Tip>
      Icon customization is platform-dependent. If this feature isn't supported on your operating system, you can skip this step.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure New Tab Mode">
    Choose how new tabs should open in Flow Browser:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Command Palette (Omnibox)">
        New tabs open with the command palette (omnibox) focused, allowing you to immediately start typing to search or navigate. This is the default and recommended mode for power users.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Page">
        New tabs open with a traditional new tab page interface, providing a visual starting point for your browsing.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    You can change this setting later in **Settings > General > New Tab Mode**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="You're All Set!">
    Once you complete all the steps, you'll see a success screen confirming that Flow Browser is ready to use.

    Click **Start Browsing** to open the main browser window and begin your privacy-focused browsing experience.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Restarting Onboarding

If you want to go through the onboarding process again:

1. Open **Settings** (Cmd/Ctrl + ,)
2. Navigate to **General** section
3. Find the **Reset Onboarding** card
4. Click the reset button to restart the onboarding wizard

<Warning>
  Restarting onboarding will not delete your existing spaces, settings, or browsing data. It simply allows you to reconfigure your initial preferences.
</Warning>

## What Happens After Onboarding?

After completing the onboarding:

- Your first space is created and activated
- Flow Browser opens with your selected settings
- You can immediately start browsing with privacy-focused features enabled
- All customizations can be modified later in Settings
